Poké Pong

How to Play

Trainer 1: W / S keys or drag left side

Trainer 2: Arrow Up / Down or drag right side

Press Space/Enter or tap Start to launch the Poké Ball.

First to 7 points takes the badge! Tap the court on tablets.

Tips

Send tricky angles by hitting with the top or bottom of your Pokémon.

The Poké Ball speeds up after every rally—stay sharp!

Press Start to release the Poké Ball.
